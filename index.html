What to Do

  1. Click on a square to fill in the numbers as seen in the puzzle

  2. Hint button: Fills in the empty squares one box at a time

  3. Solve button: Fills in the whole board

  4. Reset button: Starts a new puzzle

  5. Restart button: Starts over
